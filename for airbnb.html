THE MAKING OF EUNIKE STUDIO @BINTARO

I bought a studio apartment in October 2014 almost on impulse, without knowing what to do with it and how to pay for it. A year later, I was tired of paying the monthly bills for an empty apartment that I couldn't enjoy. 

I finally came up with an idea to make the apartment work for me, instead of the other way around. I decided to turn it into a unique, Instagrammable space that I would be proud of, and with that I can rent it out so I can pay my bills. After obsessive planning, renovating and decorating on a very tight budget, I finally have my dream studio that has it all - and people are also loving it! 

The location is far from ideal, and I had doubts whether or not people are willing to book it.  After hosting more than 70 guests in a little over a year, I found out that most people did not mind the apartment location when booking a place, as they were blown away by the room design!
