ABOUT THE STUDIO

The studio was open for public in March 2016 as an Airbnb listing; a room for rent for travelers from around the world. Its smart and unique design makes the 19 sqm space almost double its size. There is a cozy queen sized bed that could be turned into a sofa or diminished completely in day time; a comfy wooden "podium" as an all-in-one working space, dressing room, and hangout place; a cute kitchenette and dining space, and a bright bathroom.

After gaining popularity really quickly in Airbnb, it has been used as a mini studio for a conceptual fashion photoshoot, prewedding/engagement photoshoot, magazine photoshoot, and for filming some YouTube videos by a well-known online personality; not to mention numerous people posting in Instagram with the studio as the background.
