ABOUT TANDA CREATIVES

Tanda Creatives (or just Tanda) is a modern, multi-disciplinary and cooperative Agency that brings together professionals, thinkers, leaders, enthusiasts and pioneers to the creative world of designing and transforming the spaces that we live, work and play in.  

We are community-based, and rely on a crowd-sourced pool of committed and passionate subject matter experts who hold common values and goals. 

Beyond financial bottom lines, we seek to better the world though the services that we provide and the works that we do!

We believe in building a better world through Design. 

Let's grow a sustainable future together!
