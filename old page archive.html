THE STORY BEHIND THE MAKING OF EUNIKE STUDIO @BINTARO

I bought a studio apartment in October 2014 almost on impulse, without knowing what to do with it and how to pay for it. A year later, I was tired of paying the monthly bills for an empty apartment that I couldn't enjoy because I had no money to spare for the renovation and interior design. 

I finally came up with an idea to make the apartment work for me, instead of the other way around. I would turn it into a unique, Instagrammable space that I can rent out for people so I can pay my bills. After two months of obsessive planning, renovating and decorating on a very tight budget, I became a DIY master and ultra thrift shopper. I managed to locate the best and cheapest suppliers for things that people pay for much more. The result was my dream studio that had it all - half the price of what a professional interior design company would have cost me.

I am now part of TandA Creatives, and together we combine our unique skills sets and shared passion in creating a better space for everyone; so you could have a  unique space that speaks your personality yet do not have to break the bank or spend hundreds of hours planning and hunting for the best deals! 

Dream (home)s do come true. I've made mine come true, so why not yours?
